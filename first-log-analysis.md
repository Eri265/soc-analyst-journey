# First Log Analysis Report

## Objective
To understand what logs are and how they help in cybersecurity.

## What are Logs?
Logs are records of events that happen in a system. They show activities like logins, errors, and system changes.

## Why Logs Matter
Logs help SOC analysts detect suspicious activities such as unauthorized access or malware behavior.

## Basic Example
If a user logs in at an unusual time or from a different location, logs can help identify this as suspicious.

## Conclusion
Logs are very important in cybersecurity because they help detect and investigate security incidents.
